# Game Overview

![Broken Arrow Battlefield](https://picsum.photos/800/300?random=1)
*Modern combined-arms warfare in Broken Arrow*

Welcome to **Broken Arrow**, a real-time tactical military simulation that puts you in command of modern combined-arms warfare. This guide will introduce you to the core concepts and mechanics that make Broken Arrow unique.

## What is Broken Arrow?

![Team Coordination](https://picsum.photos/600/250?random=2)
*5v5 multiplayer tactical coordination*

Broken Arrow is a **team-based multiplayer strategy game** where two teams of 5 players each command modern military forces in intense tactical battles. Unlike traditional RTS games, Broken Arrow emphasizes:

- **Realistic modern warfare** with authentic military units and tactics
- **Team coordination** requiring all 5 players to work together
- **Deck-building mechanics** where you customize your battlegroup before battle
- **Complex damage systems** with armor penetration and critical effects
- **Advanced detection** and electronic warfare systems

## Core Game Concepts

### Team-Based Warfare
- **5v5 multiplayer** battles with automatic matchmaking
- **Team coordination** is essential for victory
- **Communication tools** including markers and voice chat
- **Shared objectives** that require coordinated assaults

### Battlegroups (Decks)

![Battlegroup Composition](https://picsum.photos/700/300?random=3)
*Army customization and deck building interface*

- **Pre-built army compositions** chosen before battle
- **300+ unique units** with thousands of customization options
- **Specialization-based** rosters with different military doctrines
- **Point-based system** limiting your available forces

### Victory Conditions
You can win a match by:
1. **Capturing all objectives** simultaneously
2. **Controlling more objectives** than the enemy when the 45-minute timer expires

## Key Game Mechanics

### Supply and Logistics

![Supply Operations](https://picsum.photos/600/200?random=5)
*Logistics and resupply in the field*

- **Ammunition and fuel** are finite resources
- **Supply trucks** must establish forward supply points
- **Resupply operations** are vulnerable but essential
- **Napoleon's wisdom:** "Amateurs talk strategy. Professionals talk logistics."

### Electronic Warfare
- **Radar systems** provide enhanced detection but expose you to anti-radiation missiles
- **SEAD operations** (Suppression of Enemy Air Defenses) are crucial
- **Stealth and concealment** mechanics affect detection

### Damage and Armor

![Armor Penetration](https://picsum.photos/650/250?random=6)
*Advanced damage modeling and critical systems*

- **Two damage types:** Kinetic and Explosive
- **Range-dependent penetration** for kinetic projectiles
- **Critical damage effects** can disable specific vehicle systems
- **Active Protection Systems** on advanced vehicles

## Game Flow

### Pre-Battle
1. **Join or create** a 5-player lobby
2. **Select your nation** and specialization
3. **Choose your battlegroup** (or use default)
4. **Ready up** and search for opponents

### Battle Phase

![Tactical Command](https://picsum.photos/700/280?random=7)
*Real-time battlefield command and control*

1. **Deploy reinforcements** using the reinforcement menu
2. **Coordinate with teammates** using markers and chat
3. **Control objectives** while maintaining supply lines
4. **Adapt tactics** based on enemy movements

### Post-Battle
- **Review statistics** and performance
- **Modify battlegroups** based on lessons learned
- **Join Discord** to find regular teammates

## Essential Tips for New Players

![Training Exercise](https://picsum.photos/600/220?random=8)
*Learning the fundamentals of modern warfare*

### Start Simple
- **Use default battlegroups** for your first few matches
- **Focus on communication** with your team
- **Learn the UI** before worrying about advanced tactics

### Communication is Key
- **Join the Discord server** for voice communication
- **Use markers** (F1-F4) to coordinate with teammates
- **Be courteous** in chat - toxic behavior will result in bans

### Learn from Experience
- **Test, fail, adapt, overcome** - experimentation is encouraged
- **Don't be afraid** to try new tactics during the beta
- **Ask for help** - the community is welcoming to newcomers
